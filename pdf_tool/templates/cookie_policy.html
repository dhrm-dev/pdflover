What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve functionality, remember preferences, and enhance user experience.

How We Use Cookies

  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand visitor behavior
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Performance Cookies: Monitor performance and reliability

Third-Party Cookies

Cookie Management

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy & security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions
